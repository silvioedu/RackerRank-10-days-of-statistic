Question: In a single toss of 2 fair (evenly-weighted) six-sided dice, find the probability that their sum will be at
          most .

Total Combinations............: 36
Combinations GREATER than 9...: 06    -> (4,6) (6,4) (5,5) (6,5) (5,6) (6,6)
Remain possibilities..........: 30

Solution......................: 30/36
(simplifying by 5)............:  5/6
